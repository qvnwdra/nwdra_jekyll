Q&A with MP Peter Julian and AGM

The Residents Association will be hosting a Q&A with our Member of Parliament, Peter Julian, followed by our AGM.

Join us at 7:00pm, Wednesday April 9, at the Anvil Centre, Level 4, 777 Columbia St. (Note new venue)

Please register at our event page to ensure a seat.

Agenda

  • 6:35pm: Registration
  • 7:00pm: Q&A with Mr Julian
  • 8:30pm: AGM and election of new Board

Call for volunteers:Please consider volunteering for a board position if you are interested in helping us organize public meetings and neighbourhood events. Residents with marketing and/or social media skills are especially needed.

Administrative Details

Attendance is free for NWDRA members (everyone on our mailing list) and for residents who live in Downtwown New Westminster. Everyone else is welcome to attend. We request a voluntary donation of $10.00 from non-residents to offset costs. (If you attend, you can join our maiing list and attend future meetings at no cost!)

Please arrive at least 15 minutes before the meeting starts so that we can verify membership and register new attendees.

Registration is required. Please print out or record your registration code or QR code and bring it with you.
