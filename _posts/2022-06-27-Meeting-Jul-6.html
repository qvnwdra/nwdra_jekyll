The next Public Meeting will take place on Wednesday July 6, 7pm, at the Holy Trinity Parish Hall, 514 Carnarvon St.

All Downtown New Westminster residents are invited to our Public Meeting on Wednesday July 6, 7:00pm at the Holy Trinity Cathedral Parish Hall.

Agenda

  • 6:30pm: Registration, annual membership fee collection (Voluntary donation of $5.00 per family per year)
  • 7:00pm: Update and Q&A with Jennifer Whiteside, MLA, New Westminster
  • 8:00pm: Presentation regarding 810 Agnes St redevelopment

If you live in a condo, please consider downloading a copy of the meeting notice and posting it in your building.
