--- layout: post title: "Online Meeting June 10 - 809 Carnarvon St Redevelopment" date: 2024-06-05 11:00:00 -0700 published: 2024-06-05 11:01:00 -0700 comments: false categories: meetings tags: [meeting online] --- The New Westminster Downtown Residents Association will be hosting a presentation and Q&A with Reliance Properties regarding the re-zoning request for 800 Carnarvon St.

Join us at 7:00pm, Monday June 10.

Reliance Properties has been advancing the work on a rezoning application for a project located at the intersection on Eighth St and Carnarvon St. The project consists of a 44 storey residential tower with 469 units, 6 CRU's with a total of 10,000sf of retail along Eighth St with outdoor patio space, a hotel with 145 rooms fronting Carnavon, and a dog park on the intersection of Blackie and Agnes St.

Further Information on the project can be found here: https://www.beheardnewwest.ca/809-carnarvon

The goal of the meeting would be for Reliance Properties to present the project and gather feedback from all the attendees.
