The Candidates meeting with New West Progressives, followed by the NWDRA Annual General Meeting will take place on Wednesday October 5, 7pm, at the Holy Trinity Parish Hall, 514 Carnarvon St.

All Downtown New Westminster residents are invited to our Annual General Meeting on Wednesday October, 5, 7:00pm at the Holy Trinity Cathedral Parish Hall.

Agenda

  • 6:30pm: Registration, annual membership fee collection (Voluntary donation of $5.00 per family per year)
  • 7:00pm: Vision statement and and Q&A with New Westminster Progressives Candidates
  • 8:30pm: Annual General Meeting and Board Election

If you live in a condo, please consider downloading a copy of the meeting notice and posting it in your building.
