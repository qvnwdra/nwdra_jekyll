The 2021 AGM will take place at the Holy Trinity Parish Hall, 519 Carnarvon St.

All Downtown New Westminster residents are invited to our AGM on Wednesday September 15, 7:00pm at the Holy Trinity Cathedral Parish Hall. All membership renewals are due.

Agenda

  • 6:30pm: Registration and Membership Renewals
  • 7:00pm: Keynote presentation by Wendy Johnson, Board VP, New Westminster Hospice Society
  • 7:30pm: Election of new Board
  • 7:45pm: General Discussion

All attendees are expected to be fully vaccinated. Please do your part to avoid further lockdowns!
