The next Public Meeting will take place on Wednesday September 7, 7pm, at the Holy Trinity Parish Hall, 514 Carnarvon St.

All Downtown New Westminster residents are invited to our Public Meeting on Wednesday September, 7:00pm at the Holy Trinity Cathedral Parish Hall.

Agenda

  • 6:30pm: Registration, annual membership fee collection (Voluntary donation of $5.00 per family per year)
  • 7:00pm: Vision statement and and Q&A with Patrick Johnstone and other Community First New West Candidates

If you live in a condo, please consider downloading a copy of the meeting notice and posting it in your building.
